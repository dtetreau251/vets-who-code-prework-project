About me

Hi! My name is David Tetreau. I would like to introduce myself, and share a little about myself. My career began in the Alabama National Guard in 2000 when I was 17. I was a Military Policeman and continued that role by joining the United States Army in 2002. I served in several combat areas including Djibouti, Kuwait, Iraq, and Afghanistan. When I left the Army, I started a career in law enforcement. I have been a Deputy Sheriff for 14 years and have had many rewarding experiences. As part of that role, I teach school children lessons on topic such as drug prevention. I love teaching! I am also a lifelong learner. That is why I have started learning web development. I have completed some basic lessons from several internet sources including freecodecamp, Codecademy, Khan Academy, Youtube, LinkedIn, V-School and more. I also obtained an Undergraduate Certificate in Software Development from University of Phoenix. I am currently seeking enrollment in the VetsWhoCode program. I feel like being around aspiring Web Developers who share similar life experiences as me is exactly what I need!
